Services

Wellcare Hospital is committed to providing a comprehensive range of healthcare services, embracing a holistic approach to patient well-being. From routine check-ups to specialized treatments, the hospital covers a breadth of medical disciplines. Services include but are not limited to:

Diagnostic Services

State-of-the-art diagnostic facilities for accurate and timely assessments, including radiology, pathology, and imaging services.

Maternity and Child Health

Comprehensive care for expectant mothers and specialized services for pediatric health.

Specialized Clinics

Well-equipped to handle specialized medical needs, including cardiology, orthopedics, obstetrics, and more.

Surgical Services

Performing a range of surgical procedures with a focus on precision and patient safety.

Primary Care

Offering routine check-ups, vaccinations, and preventive care to promote overall well-being.

Pharmacy Services

A fully stocked pharmacy offering a wide range of pharmaceuticals, ensuring that prescribed medications are readily available to patients.
